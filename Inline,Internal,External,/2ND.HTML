🌿 Save Nature, Save Future 🌍

Why Save Nature?

Nature gives us everything — air, water, food, and beauty. It’s our duty to protect it for ourselves and future generations.

🌳 Importance of Trees

  • Trees provide oxygen
  • They prevent soil erosion
  • Trees give shelter to wildlife
  • They keep our climate balanced

🌎 Simple Ways You Can Help

  1. Plant at least one tree every year
  2. Avoid plastic and use cloth bags
  3. Save water and electricity
  4. Participate in clean-up drives
